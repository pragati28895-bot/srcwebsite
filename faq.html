Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. SRP GLOBAL Pvt Ltd ka main kaam kya hai?
A. SRP GLOBAL Pvt Ltd ek Indian multi-service company hai jo tour & travel, event & wedding, transport, manpower job services aur IIBND business network provide karti hai.
Q. Kya aap sirf Sant Kabir Nagar me hi service dete hain?
A. Nahi, company ka base Sant Kabir Nagar, Uttar Pradesh me hai lekin services poore India aur selected international destinations tak di jati hain.
Q. Domestic & International tours me kya-kya include hota hai?
A. Normally packages me ticket booking assistance, hotel stay, local transport, basic sightseeing aur itinerary planning include hota hai. Exact details package ke hisab se likhe jate hain.
Q. Event & wedding management me kaunsi services milti hain?
A. Venue booking, decoration, sound & light, guest transport, stay arrangement, basic planning & coordination etc. Client ke budget ke hisab se customization possible hai.
Q. Manpower job services ka benefit kisko milta hai?
A. Hotels, events, sales field, support staff etc. ke liye company manpower provide karne ki planning karti hai – domestic level par bhi aur abroad opportunities ke liye bhi, as per tie-ups.
Q. IIBND kya hai?
A. IIBND – International Indian Business Network Development ek platform hai jahan Indian businesses ek dusre se connect hote hain, dealers / distributors / franchisee aur partners milte hain, aur national + international level par business expand karne ke chances badhte hain.
Q. Booking / service lene ke liye process kya hai?
A. Aap contact page par diye gaye mobile / WhatsApp / email par details send kar sakte hain. Team aapka requirement samajh kar quotation aur plan share karegi, phir confirm hone par booking process start hota hai.
Q. Kya aap 24×7 support dete hain?
A. Emergency travel & event support ke liye 24×7 coordination possible hai. Normal enquiry timing company policy ke hisab se mention kiya ja sakta hai.
