Privacy Policy & Terms (Basic Draft)

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact form se aapka naam, mobile number, email ID aur basic enquiry details

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Aapki enquiry ka jawab dene ke liye (tour, event, transport, job, business network etc.)

3. Data Sharing

  • Company aapka data kisi ko sell nahi karti.
  • Sirf zaroori partners (hotel, transport, visa agent etc.) ke saath limited details share ho sakti hain, jo service complete karne ke liye required hoti hain.
  • Law / government authority ke order par information provide ki ja sakti hai.

4. Payments & Refund

  • Advance & final payment ka structure har service / package ke hisab se alag ho sakta hai.
  • Cancellation & refund policy booking ke waqt clearly batayi jayegi.
  • Airline, hotel, transport vendor ki policy ke hisab se refund amount change ho sakta hai.

5. Website Content & Third-party Links

  • Website par diya gaya content informational purpose ke liye hai, changes possible hain.
  • Third-party website links (airline, hotel, map etc.) ke content ke liye SRP GLOBAL responsible nahi hai.

6. Changes to Policy

  • Company kabhi bhi privacy policy & terms update kar sakti hai.
  • Updated version website par publish hone ke baad effective mana jayega.

7. Contact for Any Concern

Agar aapko data privacy, booking terms ya kisi bhi point par doubt ho to aap contact page par diye gaye mobile / email par directly company se baat kar sakte hain.
